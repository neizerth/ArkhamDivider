**Arkham Divider** helps you prepare and print dividers for *Arkham Horror: The Card Game*. I hope it saves you a little time and hassle at the table.

I’ve relied mostly on **Chrome**. I *expected* printing prep to work on phones and in **Safari** or **Firefox** too — but I haven’t had time to **verify that reliably** everywhere.

If something acts up outside Chrome, please don’t sweat it. **Drop me a line** — feedback really helps fix rough edges. Together we can make this nicer for everyone.
